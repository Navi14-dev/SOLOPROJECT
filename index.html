DRINKS SECTION

SPRITE

Sprite is a clear, lemon-lime flavored soft drink created by the Coca-Cola Company. Sprite comes in multiple flavors, including cranberry, cherry, grape, orange, tropical, ginger, and vanilla. Ice, peach, Berryclear remix, and newer versions of the drinks are artificially sweetened.

FANTA

Fanta, the fruit flavored soft drink with numerous flavors that is owned by Coca-Cola has an incredibly interesting history. From first being created with Nazi Germany as a replacement for Coca-Cola to being remade and rebranded in Italy.

COCO-COLA

Coca-Cola is the largest beverage manufacturer and distributor in the world, one of the largest corporations in the United States, and one of the most successful brands in marketing history. Its headquarters are in Atlanta, Georgia.

COOL DRINKS

Cool drinks, commonly referred to as soft drinks, are non-alcoholic beverages typically served chilled. They come in a variety of flavors, including cola, lemon-lime, fruit flavors, and soda water. Many cool drinks are carbonated, giving them their fizzy texture. These beverages often contain sugar or artificial sweeteners, and some also include caffeine for an added energy boost.Cool drinks are popular worldwide and are often enjoyed with meals or as a refreshing treat on hot days. However, they are often criticized for their high sugar content, which can contribute to health issues like obesity and dental problems when consumed in excess. In response, there has been a rise in sugar-free or low-calorie versions of popular soft drinks. These drinks are available in bottles, cans, and even fountain machines in fast-food restaurants. Brands like Coca-Cola, fanta, and Sprite are household names, making cool drinks a significant part of the global beverage industry.

COFFEE SECTION

Black coffee

This is the simplest type of coffee drink you can make, as it comes straight out of your choice of coffee maker. It is plain coffee with nothing added.

Cold brew

Low acidity and sweet, cold brew coffee is made by adding coarsely ground beans to cold water and letting the mixture brew for several hours. You can also make a cold brew mocha!

Iced coffee

Unlike cold brew, iced coffee is chilled hot coffee. Refrigerate your freshly brewed black coffee, add ice and maybe a splash of cream or sugar, and there you have it.

Nitrous coffee

Nitrous coffee is a fairly recent invention. Typically poured out of a tap like a glass of beer, nitrous coffee is cold brew with added nitrogen. This makes for a sweet, smooth drink with light carbonation.

Cappuccino

A typical cappuccino is one-third espresso, one-third hot milk, and one-third milk foam. You can also order a dry cappuccino, which will be made with less milk

Mocha

A mocha is a latte that has been sweetened with chocolate syrup or cocoa powder. You can also try adding other caramel or peppermint syrups.

Irish coffee

A classic, cozy dessert drink made by mixing hot black coffee with Irish whiskey and Baileys Irish Cream. For a more decadent drink, top with whipped cream and add flavored syrups.

COFFEE

Coffee is one of the most popular beverages in the world, known for its rich flavor and stimulating effects due to caffeine. Made from roasted coffee beans, which are seeds from the Coffea plant, coffee has been enjoyed for centuries across various cultures. It can be prepared in many different ways, including espresso, drip coffee, or cold brew, each offering a unique taste profile. Beyond its role as a wake-up drink, coffee contains antioxidants and nutrients that can offer health benefits, such as improved mental alertness and metabolism. It's also a social beverage, often enjoyed in cafes or shared among friends. Coffee's versatility extends to the variety of drinks it can create, like cappuccinos, lattes, and mochas, making it a favorite among many worldwide.
